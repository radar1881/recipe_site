Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"This chocolate recipe is a classic. It is tasty! Yum!"

DifficultyPreparationCookingTotal time
Easy10 min25 min35 min

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate: 200g

Butter: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Flour: 80g

Eggs: 4

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and the flower

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven for some time for 25 min
